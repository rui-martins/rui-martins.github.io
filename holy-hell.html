Holy Hell

A personal vendetta against Him leads you to bring chaos to Heaven.

The project

Holy Hell is a vertical slice also born of a college project.

There are tons of games where you slash demons so we decided to do the opposite. You're a demon who slashes angelical beings.

Since the scope was quite big (semi open world), our 3D artist was solely focused on concept art and animations, which opened the opportunity for me to improve my 3D skills. I created almost all of the 3D models (except for some enemies and the main character).

My roles

  • Gameplay programming
    • - Implemented some of the main character's mechanics and enemies' AI;
    - Created some quality of life features to improve playability and game feel (ex: automatically snapping to an enemy if the sword dash hit is close enough).

  • 3D art
    • - Created all of the 3D models (except main character, its sword and some enemies).

  • Game design
    • - I was involved in the whole process of game design decisions.
