Where's my...

Find your stuff by meticulously controlling them and yourself in opposite directions.

The project

Where's my... was created in 48h hours for the Global Game Jam 2021 with the theme "Lost and Found".

Each level contains something from your daily life that you have lost and need to find. When moving the character, the item moves in the opposite direction and you need to be able to touch it in order to proceed.
