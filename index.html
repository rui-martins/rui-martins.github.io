Who am I?

A guy from sunny Lisbon, Portugal who loves programming and everything about game development.

Ever since I remember I've been crazy about games. I got my first console at five years old and I never stopped playing since that age. As a very curious person, I wanted to know the process behind creating games and that has lead me to take a Bachelor's degree in Game Development.

If half of me is made of games, the other half is made of music (especially heavy). I sing for the metal band Betraying the Martyrs and tour the world with them. It gives me a release of energy like nothing else in this life!

Things I can do!

  • Write clean code from scratch
  • Game Design
  • A bit of 3D Art
  • Work in a collaborative way
  • Learn quickly
  • Drink much coffee

    • My main focus is C# programming and Unity development but I also have some experience in working with C, JavaScript, MySQL and HTML.
