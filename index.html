Who am I?

A guy from sunny Lisbon, Portugal who loves programming and everything about game development.

Ever since I remember I've been crazy about games. I got my first console at five years old and I never stopped playing since that age. As a very curious person, I wanted to know the process behind creating games and that led me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Game Development.

If half of me is made of games, the other half is made of music (especially heavy). I sing for the metal band Betraying the Martyrs and tour the world with them. It gives me a release of energy like nothing else in this life!

Things I can do!

  • Work in a collaborative way

    • My main focus is C# programming and Unity development. I love to code gameplay features and tools.
    I have a good knowledge of SOLID principles and Design Patterns and try to apply those as much as possible to prevent unorganized code and obtain a fluid development when projects start to get big.

    I always aim to write the fastest, most reusable and cleanest code (with the use of interfaces, inheritance, generic methods, lambdas, etc.)
