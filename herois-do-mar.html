The project

Her贸is do Mar was one of my projects created during the second year of college.

My group wanted to create a player-versus-player game since we had never done it before. The idea of making an arena brawler came instantly but we had to give it a twist so we could make it more interesting and unique. That's when we decided to create a game filled with portuguese elements.

All the characters are portuguese historic figures. Every ability has something to do with a personality trait or something that actually happened. And of course, the arena is filled with things that resemble portuguese history (even the power-ups).

The game was one of the chosen finalist indies for 2019's Indie X and because of that, we had the opportunity to showcase it at Moche XL Games World. Needless to say that it was a huge success and everyone had a blast, since it was a portuguese event and everybody understood the references.

My roles

  • Gameplay programming
    • All of the gameplay was implemented by me.

  • Menus programming

  • Game design

  • Sound design
    • Tweaked some free copyright sounds and created others for menus and in-game. Inserted all into the game.
