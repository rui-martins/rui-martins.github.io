Happy New Rocket!

Fast paced online game where you battle for the best firework before time runs out.

The project

Happy New Rocket! was born during the Weekly Game Jam 181.

Me and my college teammates graduated in the summer of 2020 and in december 2020 we got together again to participate in a Game Jam.

The four of us had already improved our skills, hence why we decided to go the extra mile with this project.
Having no previous networking experience and being used to do jams only in 2D, we decided to challenge ourselves and build a 3D online game from scratch in just 6 days.

My roles

  • Networking programming
    • I implemented all of the networking aspects of the game. I used Photon 2 as a networking library and used the Smooth Sync asset for a more accurate synchronization of objects.

  • Gameplay programming
    • Most of the gameplay was implemented by another team member, however, I still managed to mess with some features.

  • Game design
    • I was involved in the whole process of game design.

  • About
    Happy New Rocket! is an online multiplayer game where you have to fill up crates with as many fireworks as you can.
